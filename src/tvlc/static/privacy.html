Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At veedeeoh., we prioritize user privacy above all else. Our platform is designed from the ground up to operate with zero intrusive data tracking, zero third-party profiling cookies, and maximum user transparency.

1. Zero Tracking & Zero Data Retention

We do not track your watching behavior, search queries, or viewing history. When using the web platform or self-hosting local instances of veedeeoh:

  • No personal identification information (PII) is sold or shared.
  • No behavioral profiling or persistent cross-site tracking cookies are stored.
  • Your local viewing state remains strictly stored within your browser's private LocalStorage.

2. Client-Side Video Streaming Architecture

All video stream segment downloads (.m3u8 manifests and .ts media chunks) execute directly between your web browser client and official public content distribution networks (CDNs). Your video playback bandwidth does not pass through intermediate servers or track your activity.

3. Self-Hosting & Local Installation

For users utilizing the self-hosted edition of veedeeoh via Docker, Python, or desktop releases, 100% of network requests, session configurations, and local index files reside exclusively on your local hardware.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use LocalStorage exclusively to persist essential application state such as UI preferences, active playback position, and volume settings. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

5. Contact & Open Source

veedeeoh is built with transparency in mind. If you have questions regarding privacy or architecture, please review our open-source codebase on GitHub.
